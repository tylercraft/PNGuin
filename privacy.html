Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2025  ·  PNGuin Chrome Extension

PNGuin does not collect, store, or transmit any data — ever. Everything happens locally on your device.

What PNGuin does

PNGuin is a Chrome extension that captures screenshots of web pages and saves them as PNG files directly to your computer's Downloads folder. No account is required. No server is involved.

Data we collect

None. PNGuin does not collect any personal information, browsing history, page content, or usage data.

Permissions and why we need them

  • activeTab — to capture the current tab when you trigger a screenshot.
  • contextMenus — to add the PNGuin options to your right-click menu.
  • debugger — to measure full page dimensions and capture beyond the visible viewport for full-page screenshots. No page content is read or stored; the debugger session is opened and closed solely for the screenshot.
  • downloads — to save the PNG file to your Downloads folder.

Third parties

PNGuin does not share data with any third party because no data is collected. The extension makes no network requests.

Children's privacy

PNGuin does not collect information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at pnguin.dev/privacy.html with a revised effective date.
